Profile

Xlink Technologies is a Not for Profit grassroot organization. The organization was founded by a group of young and ambitious graduates who had just completed their university studies

.

The idea of establishing the organization was informed by the existing gap in the sustainability of projects and the general lack of livelihood opportunities. The objectives of the organization are: a) To provide solutions to various programing languages; b) To offer technical support in the fields of Information and Communication Technology, c) Provide writing and research solutions, and c) Offer training and inspirational talks in schools to enable every one have equal opportunity for success inlife.
